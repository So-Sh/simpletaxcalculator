Talha Mansoor, FCCA
Title: Lead Financial Reviewer

Talha is a Fellow of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (FCCA) and a certified Financial Modeling & Valuation Analyst (FMVA) with over 15 years of experience in statutory compliance, financial controllership, and international accounting across more than 30 jurisdictions.

Career history: Audit Executive at Ernst & Young, Statutory Compliance Accountant at Google, Statutory Compliance Manager at Pure Storage, International Financial Controller at Cardinal Health — giving him direct experience with multi-jurisdiction tax compliance, statutory audit, and financial reporting under IFRS, US GAAP, and multiple local GAAP frameworks.

Role on this site: Reviews the financial logic, tax assumptions, and calculation methodologies used across the calculator suite, ensuring rate data and formulas reflect real-world tax practice.

LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/talha-mansoor-fcca-fmva

Byline scope: Interpretive and advisory tax content — anything explaining what a tax rule or number means for the reader, methodology behind a calculation, or comparisons between tax treatments. His credentials are the primary E-E-A-T signal for the site's YMYL content and should anchor any post that could be read as financial guidance.

Ray Alamian
Title: Data Research Contributor

Ray contributes to tax data collection, source verification, and structured data management for the platform. His background includes data reporting systems, compliance processes, and operational data analytics, with experience at Siemens Energy and Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy.

Role on this site: Cross-references tax rate data against official Department of Revenue publications, identifies rate changes, and maintains the accuracy of state and county-level datasets.

LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/ray-alamian-990b1080

Byline scope: Data and research-driven content — rankings, comparisons, "X by the numbers" posts grounded in public datasets (e.g. Census/Tax Foundation data already used in property-tax.json). Bio copy should reflect data research and source verification specifically; do not imply tax advisory expertise he doesn't hold. Not a fit for interpretive or advice-adjacent content.

Saeed Davari
Title: Technical Architect & Founder

Saeed is the founder of simpletaxcalculator.app and a senior software developer and solutions architect, responsible for the technical architecture, calculation logic, infrastructure, and ongoing maintenance of the platform.

LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/saeed-davari-3a218511b

Byline scope: Founder/builder content — how the platform works, data pipeline methodology, product decisions and rationale, behind-the-scenes posts. This is also a distinct link-earning channel (developer, indie-hacker, and tool-directory audiences) separate from the finance-content audience Talha and Ray's posts reach.

Not a fit for: interpretive tax-advice content or content positioned as financial guidance — a developer/architect byline carries no E-E-A-T weight there and risks weakening trust signals on YMYL pages where reader trust depends on demonstrated financial/tax expertise, not technical credentials.
